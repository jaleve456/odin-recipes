Churro Recipe

Home

Description


This is a step-by-step recipe for making churros at home. Churros are a fried dough pastry that originate from Spain and Portugal. It is a delicious treat that can be made for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of White Sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 tablespoons of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 cup of All Purpose Flour
  • 2 quarts oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup of White sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of Gound Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all of your Ingredients
  2. Combine water, 2 1/2 tablespoons of sugar, salt and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a small saucepan and place over medium heat. Bring to a boil and remove from the heat
  3. Stir in the flour and stir until the mixture forms a ball
  4. Heat oil for frying in a deep fryer or deep fryer pot to 375 defrees F (190 degrees C). Transfer the dough to a sturdy pasty bag filled with a medium star tip
  5. Carefully pipe a few 5 to 6-inch strips dough into the hot oil; work in batches so you don't crowd the fryer
  6. Cook until golden; use a spider or slotted spoon to transfer churrps to paper towels to drain
  7. Combine 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon and sugar mixture
