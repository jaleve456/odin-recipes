Haitian Kremas Recipe

Home

Description

No Haitian holiday is complete without kremas- so we're sharing a recipe to get the party started!

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Evaporated Milk
  • 2 Cans of Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 Can of Cream of Coconut
  • 1 Teaspoon of Grated Nutmeg
  • 1 Teaspoon of Cinnamon
  • 1 Teaspoon of Anise Star Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon of Almond Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon of Lime Juice
  • 1 Cup of Dark Rum

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and cream of coconut
  2. Add vanilla, almond, anise star, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lime juice, then mix again
  3. Add the rum then mix again
  4. Chill for 3-4 hours, then serve and enjoy!
